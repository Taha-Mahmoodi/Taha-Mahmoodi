## About

I think in brand and positioning before I think in code, which is
probably why PIIIX exists: most "solved" markets have a real gap once you
look at them as a brand problem, not just a technical one. I design the
interfaces, write the copy, and build the product myself when a small
team needs to move fast rather than wait on four different specialists.
PIIIX is where all of that lands.

Open-source enthusiast. Builder first, title second.
